BANGKOK POST FOUNDATION

In terms of its social contribution, The Bangkok Post Foundation, formed in 1982, has provided financial support without ceasing throughout the years to underprivileged children throughout the country for kindergarten, primary and secondary education through colleges and university studies.

"INSPIRING STORIES"

Over the past 30 years since its establishment, the Bangkok Post Foundation has sponsored approximately 5,700 scholarships. The foundation is proud of the students' successes. Three former scholarship recipients are currently working with First Class Honours, Second Class Honours and outstanding grades.
